I am a macroecologist specialised in streamlining work-flows, integration, and analysis of big data. My research uses quantitative tools

future trajectories of ecosystems. My current work includes inference and analysis of ecological networks, simulation-based
frameworks for ecological network inference assessments, and soware development for climate data retrieval and aggregation.
