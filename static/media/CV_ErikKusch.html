I am a macroecologist and data scientist specialised in streamlining work-flows, integration, and analysis of big data. My research uses
quantitative tools to study ecosystem resilience, vegetation dynamics, and landscape-scale biodiversity to understand past, present,
and future trajectories of ecosystems. My current work includes inference and analysis of ecological networks, simulation-based
frameworks for ecological network inference assessments, and soware development for climate data retrieval and aggregation.
